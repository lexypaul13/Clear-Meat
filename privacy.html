Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Scanivore (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects and uses information. By using Scanivore, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Ingredient and Nutrition Data: When you scan a barcode, we process the product’s ingredient and nutritional information but do not collect or store any personal data about you.
  • Analytics (optional): If analytics are enabled, we collect anonymized usage metrics to improve app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • We generate ingredient risk classifications, nutritional context, and safer product alternatives solely from scanned product data.
  • Analytics data (if collected) is used only to improve app performance and features and is not linked to individual users.

3. Third-Party Services

No user data is shared with third parties. Any analytics services we use collect anonymized data and comply with privacy standards.

4. Children’s Privacy

Scanivore is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete such data immediately.

5. Data Retention

Because we do not collect any personally identifiable information, no personal data is retained.

6. Your Rights

If you have enabled analytics and wish to request the deletion of analytics data associated with your use of Scanivore, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page, and the "Last updated" date above will be revised accordingly.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@scanivore.com.
